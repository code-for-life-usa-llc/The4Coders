WHAT IS UNEMPLOYMENT?

Unemployment is a situation where a person actively searches for employment but is unable to find work.

THE 3 TYPES OF UNEMPLOYMENT

cyclical structural frictional
a increase in unemployment when the economy slows down during a business cycle long-lasting unemployment that comes about due to shifts in an economy Entering the workforce for the first time
demand for goods and services decreases, which leads to a lack of jobs unemployment that results from a mismatch between labor supply and demand. Moving to a new city for work
Cyclical unemployment makes it hard for people to find jobs and there are less roles available someone getting laid off because more people are using something else Quitting a job to pursue another
